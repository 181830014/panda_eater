The same can be said for all the other em operations that take a map of properties.

E.g.

em.setProperty("javax.persistence.cache.retrieveMode", CacheRetrieveMode.USE);
em.setProperty("javax.persistence.cache.storeMode", CacheStoreMode.BYPASS);

Map map = new Properties();
map.put("javax.persistence.cache.retrieveMode", CacheRetrieveMode.BYPASS);
Employee emp = em.find(Employee.class, 1, map);

On the find, the storeMode set on the EM will be ignored/overridden and the default for the property "USE" will take effect.
