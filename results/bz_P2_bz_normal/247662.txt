Steps To Reproduce:
1. Invoke EntityManager.merge() a non-persistent entity with non-persistent associations.
2. EclipseLink will try merging all associations as well, even though JPA defines the default cascade as "none".

More information:
See the related discussion here: http://www.nabble.com/merge()-cascading-by-default--td19501823.html

There are no known workarounds.
