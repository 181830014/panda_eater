Testing small PingPong example (V1) provided by Peter Cigehn.

The log ports for Pinger and Ponger did not have type set to UMLRTLogProtocol.

This prevented successful build.

Workaround
________________

Step 1.
On the root package element, select "Import Registered Package"
Select "UML-RT Runtime Services"

Step 2.
Open the Pinger diagram.
Drag the UMLRTLogProtocol (from the RTS model library) onto the log port.


Step 3.
Open the Ponger diagram.
Drag the UMLRTLogProtocol (from the RTS model library) onto the log port.
