Currently, property jdbc.driver.jar in test.properties is used to define jdbc driver jar, it is referred by Ant projects to construct classpaths as below.
<pathelement path="${jdbc.driver.jar}"/>

It won't work out for a database platform that has more than one jdbc driver jar. For instance, JDBC driver and its license for DB2 are in db2jcc.jar and db2jcc_license_cu.jar respectively.
