Installing Ease Modeling Components 0.4.0.I201610120904 implies installing several of Papyrus' infrastructure components; which in turn triggers a flurry of SWTExceptions from Papyrus:

https://bugs.eclipse.org/bugs/show_bug.cgi?id=510150

To reproduce:

Install Eclipse Neon.2 Modeling
Install Ease Modeling Components

After restart, there are lots of SWTExceptions as shown here: https://bugs.eclipse.org/bugs/show_bug.cgi?id=510150#c2

I experienced this problem on MacOSX; however, the original report https://bugs.eclipse.org/bugs/show_bug.cgi?id=510150 is based on a Windows installation, not of EASE per se but of Papyrus.
